01 · Our Story

Any home.
Intelligently.

AYDAR was born from a simple observation — smart homes existed, but they felt foreign to Moroccan homes. Too expensive, too complex, not built for how we actually live.

The name says everything. AYDAR — Ay Dar — means any home in Darija. Because every home, regardless of size or budget, deserves to be smart.

We combine OEM hardware partnerships, local installation expertise, and a custom app to deliver the complete smart home experience — in your language.

Abir Ismaili
Founder · AYDAR
"I named it after my initials because AI is at the core of everything we do — and Dar is home. Dar-K but Smarter isn't just a slogan, it's a promise."
